About Me

Academics

Accomplishments

As a TSA National Qualifier in Forensic Science and MetroFinalist in LD Debate, I thrive in competitive academic environments that demand both critical thinking and communication.

Advocacy

My volunteer work through the American Cancer Society and VHSL has shown me the power of youth-led advocacy in raising awareness and supporting community health initiatives.

Athletics

I run varsity cross country, which has helped me develop discipline, resilience, and the importance of a team mindset. Endurance in sport mirrors my dedication in academics and service.

Passion

From neuroscience and AI-powered research to writing and debate, I’m driven by curiosity. I care deeply about solving real-world health problems with innovative tools and big-picture thinking.

Work Experience
