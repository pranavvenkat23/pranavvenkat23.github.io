Academics

I attend Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology (TJHSST), a premier STEM magnet school where I pursue advanced science and research coursework.


My academic focus lies in the intersection of neuroscience, biology, and computational science. I have completed or am currently enrolled in:

  • AP Biology – foundation for competition biology and medical research
  • AP Computer Science A+ – algorithms and data structures in Java
  • DNA Science – molecular tools, PCR, cloning, and DNA barcoding
  • Design & Technology – engineering, CAD, and prototyping
  • Neurobiology & Bionanotech (upcoming) – specialty courses for TJ seniors

I also independently study through competitive outlets such as USABO and plan to take part in JSHS and ISEF with original research. My academic journey is driven by curiosity, rigor, and the desire to build solutions that impact health and society.
